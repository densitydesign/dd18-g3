HOW TO READ PLANT FORWARD
SCROLL UP  from the bottom to follow the process of reforestation. Each dot represent an image.
The growth process is divided into 6 phases. The icon on the cursor bar indicates the phase the cursor is on.
A thicker outline indicates that the image depict (also) people.
From the bar on the right you can filter those images only.
Use the bar on the right to set your visualisation preferences.
